ABOUT US

St. Peter the Apostle School is a Chinese-Filipino educational institution founded on December 22, 1974, by Fr. Vincent Liu, a diocesan priest from New York. Guided by the RCAMES vision, we, St. Peter the Apostle School (SPAS), as a Catholic Chinese-Filipino educational institution, envision being a center of excellence for the holistic formation of learners in the service of God and country.
